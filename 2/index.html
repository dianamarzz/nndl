Understanding Correlation in Titanic Data:

In the Titanic dataset, certain features typically show strong correlations:

  • Gender (Sex): Usually shows the strongest positive correlation (females survived more)
  • Passenger Class (Pclass): Higher classes (1st class) had better survival rates
  • Fare: Higher fares correlate with better survival chances
  • Age: Younger passengers tended to survive more often
